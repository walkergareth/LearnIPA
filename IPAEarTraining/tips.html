• If you encounter problems, it would be a good idea to check that your browser has JavaScript enabled, and that you have QuickTime Player installed (download it without charge from Apple's website).
  • If you are trying to open the pages on a Mac with Firefox and Quicktime and having problems with the video, try setting Firefox to open in 32-bit mode. To do this close Firefox, navigate to the Firefox icon in Finder, press CMD-i to open Firefox info (or right-click on the icon), check the "Open in 32-bit mode" option, restart Firefox and retry.
